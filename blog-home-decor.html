🛠 Application Tips for Wall Stickers

🧹

Clean First

Wipe the wall with a dry cloth before applying stickers for maximum adhesion.

📐

Plan Your Layout

Lay stickers on the floor first to visualize the design before committing to the wall.

🌡️

Room Temperature

Apply stickers in normal room temperature — extreme cold or heat affects adhesive.

↕️

Start from Top

Apply top-down, smoothing as you go to prevent air bubbles.
