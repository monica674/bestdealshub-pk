📐 SIZE GUIDE

Women's Clothing Size Guide for Daraz Pakistan

Never order the wrong size again. Use our complete measurement charts and fitting tips for dresses, suits, and gowns.

💡 Pro Tip: Always measure yourself before ordering. Pakistani clothing brands vary in sizing — a "Large" from one brand may fit like a "Medium" from another. When in doubt, size up!

📏 How to Take Your Measurements

🫁

Chest / Bust

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape parallel to the floor.

⌛

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline — the narrowest part of your torso.

🍑

Hips

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, about 8 inches below your waist.

📏

Length

Measure from the top of your shoulder straight down to where you want the hem to fall.

⚠️ Important Notes for Daraz Shoppers

• Stitched products are pre-sewn — check measurements carefully as alterations after purchase may not be refunded.

• Unstitched/semi-stitched fabric can be tailored to your exact measurements — more flexible.

• Embroidered gowns often have extra fabric — when in doubt, order your usual size or one smaller.

• Linen & lawn suits may shrink slightly after first wash — order one size up if between sizes.
